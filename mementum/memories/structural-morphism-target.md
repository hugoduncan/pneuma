❌ The structural morphism protocol-ops->return-types from dogfood-protocol.md §3.3 does not work as designed. It checks effect-signature operation outputs (type keywords like :Any, :MalliSchema) against a CapabilitySet's schema (an enum of operation names like :->schema, :check). These are different domains — type keywords are not operation names. The structural morphism needs a dedicated return-type schema formalism as its target, not a CapabilitySet. Removed from default registry. The existential morphism (caps->protocol/operations) works correctly.

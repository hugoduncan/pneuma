mealy-handler-set, optic-declaration, and resolver-graph constructors all take `:declarations` as a **vector** of maps (not a map keyed by id). The constructors validate against this schema then internally index by `:id`. Effect-signature takes `:operations` as a map-of keyword to operation-schema. Statechart takes `:states` as a set, `:transitions` as a vector. Optic Derived declarations require `:sources` as `[:map-of :keyword [:vector :any]]` and `:derivations` as `[:map-of :keyword :any]`.
